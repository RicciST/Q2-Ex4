Apples

Apples are a crowd favorite when it comes to fruits. This sweet fruit is very nutritious and could possibly aid in weight loss. Not only that, but apples may also decrease the risk of getting strokes, heart disease, and diabetes.



Bananas

Similar to apples, bananas are also a popular fruit. Some similarities that bananas share with apples are that they may help with weight loss, are nutritious, and are healthy for your heart. Bananas are also rich in fiber which make them great for the health of your digestive system.
